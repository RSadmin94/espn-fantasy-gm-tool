## Tone Principle

Every line should feel **observant, not dramatic**. Data-backed, not movie trailer.

- GOOD: "Christian's trade activity spikes after consecutive losses."
- BAD: "Christian falls apart under pressure."

The first is believable. The second collapses trust.
